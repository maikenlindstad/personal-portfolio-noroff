About me

I have always been an explorer and could never pinpoint the one thing to do with my life. To be honest, I wanted to do it all. As a child I played piano, tuba, trumpet and guitar. I danced jazz, ballet, breakdancing and hiphop. If I found an item, I tried it out and learned the history behind it. And when I got older I found interior design, fashion, drawing, painting, sculpting, marketing, economics ++. I wanted to visit the entire world and try everything in it. I learned a lot, and I have been keeping it up.

I ended up working in healthcare, but never found the mental stimulation that I needed. It became difficult to figure out what I wanted out of life, but after a lot of motivational speeches I found the courage to quit my job, go out there and find my path. My philosophy became to find my passion and make a living out of it, rather than start making a living and try to find my passion. After getting into college I started noticing that I liked the courses that was centered around information technology and after building my first webpage, I knew.

I was going to be a frontend developer.

Fast forward a few years and here we are. I graduated out of Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media in December 2022. Eagerly anticipating a career with a lot of variety, I placed my focus on school and was gifted the opportunity to work hard on making my personal life simple to make room for what's to come. #mariekondo #minimalism #ikigai #epicure and a little #newage.

Besides thriving with front end codes I'm also captivated by digital fashion, AI, VR and the meta universe. I love Halloween and in 2021 I started my own e-commerce selling witch- and Halloween items for other Norwegian enthusiasts. Every other Thursday since the summer of 2022 I have been vlogging for a known Norwegian witch coven, Coven Lunatic, and I play chess at my local club. Finally, when I need to take a breather I usually write or meditate, or one might find me streaming on Twitch... But I also love sitcoms.

I always dream big and hope that I will find a place with dreams as big as mine, and that my chosen path can lead me to places I have never been before.
